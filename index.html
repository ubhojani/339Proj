Michigan Basketball

History

The first season of Michigan Basketball occurred in 1909 who achieved a 1-4 record. The program was then shut down due to low attendance but it returned in 1917 in what was considered the inaugural season of varsity basketball.
The basketball program has had a total of 17 head coaches lead the program, with Steve Fisher winning the lone National Championship in 1989.
